Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Yin&Yang Studio ("we", "our", "us") operates the Kismet mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Data: When you use Kismet, an anonymous account is created automatically. No email, name, or personal identifier is required to use the app.

Birth Date (Optional): If you choose to provide your birth date for horoscope features, it is stored securely and used solely to determine your zodiac sign.

User-Generated Content: Text you enter for fortune readings (dream descriptions, questions, names) is sent to our AI processing service to generate your reading. This content is stored temporarily to display your reading and in your reading history.

Photos (Optional): For coffee cup and palm reading features, photos you upload are processed by our AI service. Photos are not stored permanently on our servers after the reading is generated.

Usage Data: We collect anonymized usage statistics (features used, reading counts) to improve the app experience.

2. AI-Powered Features

Kismet uses artificial intelligence to generate fortune readings. Our AI services are provided by:

  • Google Gemini (for free-tier readings)
  • Anthropic Claude (for premium readings)

When you request a reading, your input (text or images) is sent to these AI services through our secure server infrastructure. The AI providers process your input to generate a reading response. We do not share your personal identity with these providers — only the content necessary for generating your reading.

Important: AI-generated readings are for entertainment purposes only and should not be considered professional advice of any kind.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized fortune readings
  • To display your reading history
  • To determine your zodiac sign (if birth date is provided)
  • To track daily usage limits
  • To improve app functionality and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS infrastructure) with encryption at rest and in transit. We implement row-level security policies to ensure users can only access their own data.

Locally saved favorites are stored on your device using encrypted local storage and are not transmitted to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics: For app performance monitoring and crash reporting
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements to free-tier users. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.
  • RevenueCat: For managing premium subscriptions
  • Supabase: For secure data storage and authentication

6. Advertising

Free-tier users may see advertisements served by Google AdMob. You can watch optional reward ads to earn extra readings. Ad-related data collection follows Google's advertising policies. Premium users enjoy an ad-free experience.

7. Children's Privacy

Kismet is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Your reading history is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time through the app settings, which will permanently remove all your data from our servers within 30 days.

Locally saved favorites are removed when you uninstall the app or clear app data.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Export your data

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date above.
